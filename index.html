Celebrating Orians who are shaping what human + AI collaboration looks like.

Most of this starts small. A better workflow. A faster way to get something done. Something you share that helps someone else do their work better. The goal here isn't perfection. It's progress. These levels are just a way to make that progress visible.

AI Practitioner
"You've used AI to meaningfully improve a real workflow, and you can show the before and after."

This is where most people start. It's about applying AI in real work, not just experimenting in isolation. It doesn't need to be massive. We care about practical improvements that genuinely make your work better.

What "meaningful improvement" looks like will vary by role. For some, it's a full workflow. For others, it's eliminating a repetitive task or improving the quality of something you do every day.

If you've been experimenting with AI at all, there's a good chance you're closer to one of these than you think.
